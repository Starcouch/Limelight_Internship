The Big Three of Anime

The Big Three refers to Naruto, One Piece, and Bleach — the three legendary anime series that dominated global popularity during the 2000s and shaped modern shonen storytelling.

Naruto

Naruto follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja striving to become Hokage. With over 220 episodes, the series became a defining anime for a generation.

One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his crew on a quest to find the legendary treasure. With over 1000 episodes, it is one of the longest-running and most successful anime series ever.

Bleach

Bleach centers on Ichigo Kurosaki, who gains Soul Reaper powers and protects the world from spiritual threats. With 366 episodes, it completes the legendary Big Three trio.
